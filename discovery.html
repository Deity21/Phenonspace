Discovery

Overview

Discovery is a story-driven VR shooter with tactical elements and world exploration built on the power of Unreal Engine 5. The game dive players into the thrilling and perilous world of Rogax –a distant planet at the end of the universe. Hunt down dangerous exotic beasts, traverse stunning landscapes, and unravel the mysteries of the planet. We promise an adrenaline-pumping, action-packed experience accompanied by a captivating narrative.

Game Tech Stack

Unreal Engine 5, Lumen, Nanite, Niagara

Our Goals

  • Create a game with mesmerizing graphics using Unreal Engine 5 technology
  • Convey a space theme using our environment art and design services
  • Make the game available for PC and console

PhenonSpace Solutions

  • Created a story-driven first-person VR shooter with tactical elements and a vast variety of enemies
  • Created a game teaser
  • Crafted spectacular, realistic visuals and rich, intricate world lore
  • Developed highly immersive gaming experience with integrated AI NPCs
  • Developed using the latest Unreal Engine 5.4 with Meta Quest 3
